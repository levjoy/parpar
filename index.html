Parpar

A Digital Security Diagnostic Tool

Instructions

The Parpar diagnostic tool will help you understand your organization's current digital security posture. Please answer each question as accurately as possible. Use the following response options:

  • Yes - Fully implemented/documented
  • Partially - Some measures in place but incomplete
  • No - Not implemented or unknown
  • N/A - Not applicable to your organization

You only need to fill out those sections that are relevant to your work - feel free to leave sections blank if they don't apply.

NOTE: To keep your data safe, this form doesn't autosave or back up to the cloud! Here are steps to save your form:

  1. Click "Print/Save as PDF"
  2. Click "Destination" in the print dialog and select "Save to PDF"
  3. Choose somewhere on your computer to save the PDF

Don't exit or refresh the page before taking these steps, or you will lose your work!
